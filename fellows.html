Frequently Asked Questions

What are the eligibility requirements?

Applicants should have at least 3 years of mathematics teaching or research experience and demonstrate innovation in educational practices.

How long is the fellowship term?

Fellowships are awarded for one academic year with the possibility of renewal based on participation and contribution.

Is there a time commitment?

Fellows are expected to participate in monthly virtual meetings and contribute to at least one collaborative project per year.

When are applications reviewed?

We review applications on a rolling basis throughout the year, with cohorts starting each semester.
