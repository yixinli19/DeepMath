The Implementation

In a Grade 8 class, the teacher implemented DeepMath as a regular part of the curriculum, scheduling three 45-minute sessions per week. This structured approach allowed students to consistently engage with the platform while maintaining their regular coursework.

"DeepMath has transformed how we teach mathematics. The systematic approach and regular practice sessions have helped our students develop a deeper understanding of mathematical concepts, leading to remarkable improvements in their grades."
- Mr. Zhang, Grade 8 Math Teacher

The Results

After one semester of implementation, the results were remarkable. Among 11 classes in the grade, this class achieved the highest rankings in multiple grade ranges:

  • ✓ Top Performance: 22% of students achieved A grade or above, ranking 1st in the grade
  • ✓ Strong Results: 54.17% achieved B grade or above, leading all classes
  • ✓ Solid Foundation: 80% achieved C grade or above, the highest percentage in the grade
  • ✓ Low Failure Rate: Only 10% of students failed, achieving the second-lowest failure rate

What makes these results even more impressive is that this class started from behind at the beginning of the semester, demonstrating the effectiveness of consistent, structured use of the DeepMath platform.

The Approach

The success was achieved through a systematic approach:

  • ✓ Regular Sessions: Three 45-minute sessions per week, ensuring consistent practice
  • ✓ Structured Learning: Integration with regular curriculum to reinforce classroom concepts
  • ✓ Progress Monitoring: Regular tracking of student performance and adjusting support as needed
  • ✓ Personalized Support: Individual attention to students based on their performance levels

This is how public schools in China utilize the system. Students come in front of the whiteboard, read the questions, and try to solve them in front of the class. By looking at the hints, it helps them understand and succeed.
